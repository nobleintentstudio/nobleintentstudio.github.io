10 Ways To Make Your Lead Generation Website Convert On The First Visit

design web design conversion optimization
April 11, 2017
by Brad Schorr

First impressions are extremely important, but they may be most important in the context of Internet marketing. That’s because websites have a very limited window in which they can make an impression on visitors and get them to convert. Straight North, a Chicago-based Internet marketing company, took the time to review more than 350,000 website conversions, and found that visitors converted on their first visit to a site more than 80 percent of the time. After that, the odds a visitor will convert drops significantly no matter how many times they return.

Given how important driving conversions is for Internet marketing, it’s key lead generation websites be optimized to drive those conversions immediately. The following presentation includes some important tips to keep in mind when designing or optimizing a site that will help it convert visitors right away.

Your first impression is the most important one, so make sure it counts.
