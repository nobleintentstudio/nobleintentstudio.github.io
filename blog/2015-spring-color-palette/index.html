Get back to nature with Pantone’s Spring 2015 Color Palette.

design
February 15, 2015
by Angela Noble

This Spring's minimalistic color palette derives its inspiration from nature. Think cooler, softer colors with subtle warm tones following an en plein air theme.

For all the details straight from the source, see Pantone’s Spring 2015 Fashion Color Report!

Marsala, the Pantone color of the year for 2015, serves as the foundation for this season's palette.

I personally love the this Spring's nature-inspired palette! My favorites are Scuba Blue and Glacier Gray.

I’ve put together an Adobe Swatch Exchange color palette which includes all of Pantone’s Spring 2015 colors. It’s free to download here.

Which color is your favorite? Leave it in the comments!
