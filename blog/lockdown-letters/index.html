Lockdown Letters

community
April 13, 2020
by Angela Noble

We know everyone could use a little more connection and touch right now. If you're tiring of video chats with your friends and family, try something a little more tangible and mail a hand-written note to your loved ones — just don't lick the envelope.



We're giving away free sets of 10 cards featuring our shimmering gold interlocking antique lock and key design on the front (blank on the inside) and blank envelopes. Just follow us on Instagram, tag a friend in the comments of our Lockdown Letters post, and message us with your name and address and we'll get your set mailed out to you.

We hope you're staying home and staying well.
