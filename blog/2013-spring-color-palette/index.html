Get a fresh start this spring with Pantone’s Spring 2013 Color Palette

design
March 20, 2013
by Angela Noble

I’ve been thinking a lot about color this week – especially vibrant, spring colors. Maybe it’s because it’s the dead of winter where I am in Chicago this week…

Spring colors suggest renewal, excitement, and a fresh start. I’m looking forward to what the next season has in store, and I’m excited about Pantone’s Spring 2013 Color Report!

I personally love the blue and green palette with pops of red and orange! Emerald, the Pantone color of the year for 2013, is also my birthstone, so it’s an especially exciting year of color for me!

I’ve put together an Adobe Swatch Exchange color palette which includes all of Pantone’s Spring 2013 colors. It’s free to download here.

Which color is your favorite? Leave it in the comments!
