Vidajuice

An easy-to-update, custom Wordpress website for juice and smoothie bar Vidajuice.

Timeline: 1-2 months

Price Range: $3-5k

Vidajuice, a Mexican-inspired juice and smoothie bar located in Kensington, came to us in need of a website before their grand opening in conjunction with the reopening of Kensington Video.

Vidajuice needed an easy-to-navigate, simple website to showcase their menu items. The website would also need to incorporate a blog where they could post information and photos from local events they attend.

We developed a modern and fun style to pair with their existing logo and color palette. Photos of families, mature couples, and fresh ingredients were sourced, and custom illustrations for all their menu categories were designed.

We created a custom theme built on Wordpress so the Vidajuice team can easily update their menu items and post blog posts.
