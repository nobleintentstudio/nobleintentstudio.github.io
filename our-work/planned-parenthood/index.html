Planned Parenthood Pacific Southwest

Custom WordPress website for Planned Parenthood Pacific Southwest's Careers Website

Timeline: 2-4 months

Price Range: $10-15k

We worked with Brandemix and lime1 Design, who led the design effort of this project, to develop a recruitment and careers website for Planned Parenthood Pacific Southwest. The site includes employee-generated content that gives potential employees realistic job previews, shows what it's like to work there, and addresses common concerns such as "will my job be secure in the current political climate?".

The site, which launched in Q3 2016, continues to see a steady up-tick in traffic over time and serves as a great referral source for potential job applicants for the organization.

Visit the website http://pppswcareers.org/
