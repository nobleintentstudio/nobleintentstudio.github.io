2015 Selenium Conference

Custom Wordpress website built in conjunction with PS Design.

Timeline: 2-3 months

Price Range: $3-5k

Design firm PS Design contacted us to help build the 2015 Selenium Conference Website. Using the Divi framework as a starting point, we built a completely customized Wordpress theme to power the site.

The site has custom sections showcasing the conference speakers, schedule of events and other conference information.

We worked closely with PS Design throughout the development process to ensure the finished site matched the design they created and the branding they designed for the 2015 conference (see it here).

"Cris was an integral part of the development of the Selenium Conference web presence. He executed our vision perfectly in a very timely manner. During the process he was very helpful and flexible about the specifics and execution of the site and its inner workings and offered valuable development recommendation. The final product came out great and served its function well to help the attendees navigate the event and learn more about the conference and its speakers."

Petre Spassov,
Art Director at PS Design
