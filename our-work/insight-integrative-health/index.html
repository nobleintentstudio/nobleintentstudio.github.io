Insight Integrative Health

Dr. Jeremy Flagel came to us as he was making plans to open a functional medicine practice with a focus on mental health in the Kensington neighborhood of San Diego.

Timeline: 4-6 months

Price Range: $6-8k

We worked with him to create a logo based on the concept that our bodies and brains are deeply connected and that reflects his approach of root-cause treatment with a focus on mental health.

After the logo was developed, we put together a stationery system and outdoor signage.

We directed a photoshoot with local photographer Chris Brake Photography to capture images to use on the new website. The photos give new patients a feel for the type of one-on-one approach they can expect with Dr. Flagel.

We then designed a built a custom website which outlines all of Insight Integrative Health's services and includes an informative blog and links to appointment scheduling.

We're confident the new and improved brand identity and website will help Dr. Flagel in establishing Insight Integrative Health as a trustworthy medical practice that provides hope and healing to patients.

View the full site at https://insight-integrative-health.com
