Friends of Franklin Foundation

Logo, branding, marketing materials, and custom Wordpress website for Friends of Franklin Foundation.

Timeline: 1-2 months

Price Range: $3-5k

The Friends of Franklin Foundation supports Franklin Elementary STEAM Magnet School which serves students from City Heights, Talmadge, and Kensington in San Diego. The Foundation aims to raise money to fund the STEAM program at the school. As the new board formed the foundation, they reached out to us to begin helping them put together a brand identity, marketing materials, and a website to process donations.

We put together a logo comprised of icons representing the STEAM program which includes science, technology, engineering, art, and math. The logo has a sophisticated and trustworthy feel with a hint of playfulness.

We designed marketing materials including postcards, remittance envelopes, and tradeshow banners they can use at fundraising and community events.

We also designed and developed a custom website for the foundation they can use to reach donors. The website features a blog where they can sure upcoming fundraisers and events, a progress bar to show their goals and impact, and a donor page where they can thank their donors.

We built the site on the Wordpress platform so the Foundation board members and volunteers can easily login to add and adjust content as necessary.
